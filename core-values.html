Core Values

Listen.Learn.Lead.

Listen to technology professionals, learn to use the technical tools at your disposal and lead the way for fellow tech-enthusiasts to make revolutionary advancements in technology .

Innovation

We expose our students to ideas that make them think about the world around them, and many of our students here have dreams of social change and entrepreneurship.

Perseverance

The quality of perseverance is especially critical to reaching long-term goals, dreams or vision for ourselves or at IOSD. Achieving goals require faith and conviction to keep us moving in the right direction.

Education

There are very few societies that enable people to discover where they can go from the beginning. IOSD works to fulfil this need. True coding requires head, heart and hand.

Service to Others

We believe these are pragmatic skills that increase a person’s ability to code confidently, focus on tasks and take initiative in finding information, it helps them once they’ve finished college.

Creativity

We use creative methods in our classrooms, and incorporate a system of learning values and spreading knowledge to family members and their communities
